About Sneh Foundation

Sneh Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities by providing access to education, healthcare, food, and shelter. We empower people through sustainable development and compassionate community programs.

Our Mission

To bring hope, dignity, and opportunities to vulnerable populations through impactful programs and dedicated service.

Our Vision

A world where every individual lives with dignity, has access to essential resources, and can pursue their dreams without barriers.
