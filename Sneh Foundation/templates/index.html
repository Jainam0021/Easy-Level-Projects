About Sneh Foundation

Where Love Meets Action

Our Story

Born from a simple belief that every human being deserves dignity, opportunity, and love, Sneh Foundation began as a small group of compassionate individuals who refused to accept that some children must go to bed hungry while others have abundance.

Today, we stand as a beacon of hope, touching thousands of lives through our comprehensive programs in education, healthcare, women's empowerment, and community development. Our journey is not just about providing aidâ€”it's about restoring hope, rebuilding dreams, and creating lasting change.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of their circumstances, has access to quality education, healthcare, and the opportunity to realize their full potential. A world where compassion bridges all divides.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities through sustainable development programs, education initiatives, healthcare access, and women's empowerment, creating a ripple effect of positive change that transforms lives and communities.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Transparency, Empowerment, and Inclusivity guide every decision we make. We believe in treating every individual with dignity and respect, regardless of their background or circumstances.
